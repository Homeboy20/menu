Pricing FAQ

Do I need a credit card for the free trial?

No! Start your 14-day free trial without entering any payment information. We only ask for a credit card if you decide to upgrade to a paid plan.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel your subscription anytime from your dashboard. Your account remains active until the end of your billing period.

What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

We'll notify you before you hit any limits. You can upgrade your plan anytime to unlock more features. No service interruptions, no overage charges.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes! Save 20% when you pay annually. Professional drops from $468/year to $372/year. Enterprise from $1,188/year to $948/year.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise customers.

Do you offer custom plans for large chains?

Yes! If you need more than 10 locations or custom features, contact our sales team for a tailored Enterprise plan.
