Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RestOrder. Can't find your answer? Contact our support team.

Getting Started

How do I sign up for RestOrder?

Click "Start Free Trial" on any page, enter your email and business details, and you're in! No credit card required for the 14-day trial. You can create your first menu in under 10 minutes.

Do I need technical skills to use RestOrder?

Not at all! RestOrder is designed for restaurant owners, not developers. If you can use email, you can use RestOrder. Our drag-and-drop menu builder makes it easy to add dishes, upload photos, and customize your menu.

How long does it take to set up my first menu?

Most restaurants have their first menu live in 10-15 minutes. Upload your items, add prices, generate a QR code, and you're done. No waiting for printing or delivery.

Can I import my existing menu?

Yes! Upload a PDF, image, or spreadsheet and we'll help you import it. Professional and Enterprise plans include assisted import—our team will do it for you.

Features

Can I customize the look and feel of my menu?

Absolutely! Professional and Enterprise plans let you customize colors, fonts, logos, and layouts. Match your restaurant's branding perfectly with no RestOrder watermark.

Do menu updates happen in real-time?

Yes—changes appear instantly on all QR codes and links. Mark an item as sold out at 7 PM, and customers see the update immediately. No reprinting, no delays.

Does RestOrder support multiple languages?

Yes! Add translations for any language. Customers click their flag icon and see your menu in English, Spanish, French, Chinese, or 50+ other languages.

Can I add photos to my menu items?

Definitely! Upload high-res images and we'll automatically optimize them for fast loading. Beautiful food photos boost orders by 30% on average.

What kind of analytics does RestOrder provide?

See which dishes customers view most, peak viewing times, popular categories, and conversion rates. Professional plans unlock advanced analytics with custom reports.

Pricing & Billing

What's included in the free plan?

One digital menu, up to 50 items, QR code generation, basic analytics, and mobile optimization. Perfect for testing RestOrder or small cafes with simple menus.

Do I need a credit card for the free trial?

No! Start your 14-day Professional trial with just an email. We only ask for payment if you decide to upgrade after the trial ends.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel from your dashboard anytime. Your account stays active until the end of your billing period, then automatically downgrades to Free.

What's your refund policy?

30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If RestOrder isn't the right fit, we'll refund your first month in full. Just email support@restorder.online.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes! Save 20% when you pay annually. Professional drops from $468/year to $372/year. Enterprise from $1,188/year to $948/year.

Technical

What devices are supported?

RestOrder works on any device with a web browser—iPhone, Android, tablets, laptops, desktop computers. No app download required. Your customers just scan the QR code.

Can customers view menus offline?

Menus require internet to load initially, but they're optimized to load in under 2 seconds. We recommend having WiFi available for customers (or they use their mobile data).

Does RestOrder integrate with my POS system?

Enterprise plans include API access for custom integrations. We work with most major POS systems (Square, Toast, Clover, etc.). Contact sales@restorder.online for details.

Is my data secure?

Yes! Bank-level encryption, GDPR compliant, SOC 2 certified. Your data is backed up daily and stored on secure servers. We'll never sell your information.

What's your uptime guarantee?

99.9% uptime SLA for Enterprise plans. Our infrastructure is hosted on AWS with automatic failover. In 2025, we achieved 99.97% uptime.

Account Management

How do I change my password?

Go to Dashboard → Settings → Security. Click "Change Password", enter your current password and new password. You'll receive a confirmation email.

Can I add team members to my account?

Yes! Professional plans allow up to 3 team members. Enterprise plans support unlimited users with role-based permissions (admin, editor, viewer).

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. We'll ask for confirmation and give you 30 days to change your mind. After 30 days, all data is permanently deleted.

Can I export my menu data?

Yes! Export your menu as JSON, CSV, or PDF anytime. Go to Dashboard → Export Data. Your data is yours—we make it easy to take it with you.
