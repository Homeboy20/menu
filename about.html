Our Story

RestOrder started with a simple restaurant problem: menus change constantly, but paper menus do not. A dish sells out, prices shift, a seasonal special arrives, or a delivery item needs to be paused, and suddenly the menu guests see is already behind the kitchen.

We built RestOrder so operators can create a polished digital menu first, test the structure, add categories and photos, and only upgrade when they are ready to put that menu in front of guests. No pressure to pay before the restaurant has seen the value.

"A restaurant should be able to build its menu for free, then go live when the QR code, ordering flow, and daily operations are ready."

The platform is designed for the realities of service: fast edits before a rush, QR menus by table or counter, online ordering, room-service QR flows, staff access, email receipts, CSV imports, payment gateways, and analytics that show what guests actually view and order.

Our goal is practical: help more restaurants reduce printing waste, keep menus accurate, capture more direct orders, and build a digital presence that feels as professional as the food they serve.
