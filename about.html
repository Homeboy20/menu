Our Story

In 2023, our founder walked into his favorite Italian restaurant and noticed something frustrating: Beautiful food, amazing service, but sticky laminated menus with outdated prices. When he asked the owner about it, she sighed: "We change our menu every season, but reprinting costs $800-$1,200 each time. So we just... live with it."

That moment sparked an idea. What if restaurants could update menus instantly? What if every table had a QR code linking to a beautiful, always-current digital menu? What if this didn't require technical expertise or massive upfront costs?

"We built RestOrder for that Italian restaurant owner. And for the 767,000 other restaurants, cafes, bars, and eateries facing the same challenge."

Within months, we launched our MVP. Within a year, we'd helped 1,247+ businesses save over $2.8 million in printing costs alone. But the real impact goes beyond savings: faster table turns, higher average orders, real-time menu updates, detailed analytics, and happier customers.

Today, RestOrder powers digital menus for restaurants from family-owned cafes to multi-location chains. We're not just a software company�we're partners in your success.
