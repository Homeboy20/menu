QR Menu for Cafes That Need Speed and Simplicity

RestOrder helps cafes replace printed menus with mobile-friendly QR menus that are easy to update during busy service windows.

Start Free

Why cafes use QR menus

  • Update pastry, brunch, and drink availability instantly.
  • Avoid reprints when prices change.
  • Improve ordering clarity during peak hours.

Best setup for a cafe floor

  • Place QR cards at each table and counter queue.
  • Feature top drinks and best-sellers at the top.
  • Use high-quality images for premium items.
